Deep Dive

Investors want to know that every dollar you raise has a job. We examine your burn multiple, the quality of capital already on the cap table, and whether your runway buys progress or simply time.

Advantage — compounding differentiation

We test how defensible your position is: patents, proprietary data, network effects, and the story your customers would tell about switching away.

M

Market — opportunity size & traction

Great teams in small ponds drown slowly. We quantify TAM, segment velocity, retention, and the depth of urgency behind your demand curve.

• Market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM) & growth rate
• Pipeline quality, conversion, and expansion
• Competitive landscape and share capture

Ideas are easy; shipping is not. We read founder-market fit, the operating rhythm of the team, and how quickly insights become deployed product.

• Team composition, experience, mission alignment
• Speed of iteration, learning velocity, operating cadence
• Advisory bench and talent magnetism
